💡 Training Tips

  • GPU Acceleration: Enable CUDA for 5-10x faster training. See docs/CUDA_SETUP.md
  • Batch Size: Larger batches need more GPU memory but train faster
  • Epochs: Start with 50-100 epochs, increase if accuracy plateaus
  • Learning Rate: Lower rates = slower convergence, higher rates = risk of instability
  • Data Quality: Ensure detection logs have high-quality images and accurate labels
